CANDIDATE INFORMATION
OPENING
Hi [Name], this is Slade Smiley calling with AO Globe Life. The reason I am calling is you watched a brief overview on our company and I wanted to get to know you a little better. How are you today?
Great! When you watched the company overview you expressed interest in moving forward in the interview process.
LICENSING QUALIFICATION
✅ IF YES
That's fantastic! Having your license already puts you in a great position to hit the ground running.
❌ IF NO
Ok, let me walk you through the licensing process… We are in a highly regulated industry so it is mandated that you obtain a license in order to work with clients. However, in our industry the licensing process is much quicker than in other professions. How this works is — if we agree to begin working together, we would help you enroll in a pre-licensing course. This is a 40-hour online course and gets you certified to take the state exam. This course is typically $50–$150 and takes about a week for most people to complete.

Right after, you will meet one-on-one with a senior leader to answer any additional questions and ultimately decide if this position is a mutual fit. This interview takes about an hour.
